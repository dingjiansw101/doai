Detecting Objects in Aerial Images (DOAI)

Workshop in conjunction with CVPR 2019 June 16, 2019, Long Beach, California.

Note

  • Please be careful about the format, otherwise no assurance is guaranteed that the results can be correctly tested.
  • As the evaluation server is still improving, if you have problems using them, you can choose to send the results with correct format to Email: doai.cvpr2019@gmail.com. Please attach your Team name, Institute and team members to the email, otherwise we can not guarantee the results will be evaluated. The evaluation process executed by human takes about 2 days, please take it easy.
  • For evaluation, you must registrate and submit on the evaluation server. Your submission must include results on the whole test images in DOAI2019.
